About Us - Our Mission and Core Values

JKM Travels is one of the fastest growing leader in providing all kinds of transportation solutions to our clients. we are working with all types of organizations, including corporate, schools and colleges, wedding parties, religious congregations to helping and solving transportation requirement. Through charter bus, rental and contract transportation, we provide everything from convention shuttles to airport transportation to executive transportation. JKM travels is equipped with best of the the resources, experience to meet every transportation requirement. Best of all, our dedication to unparalleled safety and customer service has remained unchanged through the years. At JKM Travels our mission is clear: to be your single transportation solution.

Mission Statement

Our work is our passion and through our continued passion we always work hard to provide superior transportation services, and this could not be done without our friendly, relentless and dedicated team members.

Customer Service

We employ the right people and train them to deliver exceptional service. In fact, in a move unique to the industry, we train our staff to ensure the best possible service. We use our statistical models to monitor traffic incidents, mechanical failures and customer service issues to verify that our passengers have a great experience.

Safety

Safety is always taken seriously. We use screening techniques, including drug/alcohol screening, criminal background check, personality testing. Safety is not limited to the highway; we also utilize extensive safety systems for our maintenance and office environments.All our buses are equipped with GPRS systems to monitor them real time.

Reliability

Our maintenance facility and procedures are state of the art and most of our trips are completed without any mechanical problems.

Integrity

We value honesty and integrity. All of our employees must adhere to the highest standards. We do not tolerate dishonesty and will dismiss employees who are dishonest with us or our customers.

Teamwork

We view our customers as partners and focus on building long-term relationships. Whenever we are involved in planning the transportation services along with the customer we routinely suggest cost saving ideas that can be implemented without hurting customer service. Internally, we foster excellent working relationships between our employees.
